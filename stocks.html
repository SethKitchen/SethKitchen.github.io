7-Day Stock Trades using deep learning.

Start/Strategy.

Put 2,100 into Robinhood Gold Stock Trading Account.

On BUY days, pull S&P 500 tickers for the last 20 years and use DeepAR neural network to predict individually the next 7 days mean value with level of uncertainty.

Calculate the largest percent change (maxValue(7days)-startDay)/startDay * 100 for each and sort the table descending.

Invest some in each of the top percent change predictions as long as uncertainty is relatively small.

Place limit sell for half of (maxValue(7days)-startDay)+startDay.

Record whether successful or not. If after 1 month the limit sell is never reached sell anyway.

April 30, 2020

Buy Arconic (ARNC)

From this we can see Arconic is still expected to grow from 8.59 to 11.07 in the next week

Therefore I put a limit order in for $9 and the order went through at 150 @ 8.75 ($1312.50). Actually near the start of the market ARNC went as low as 8.39 which is very close to the prediction of 8.59. I could have put the limit lower.

I put a limit sell at 10.29 which the net is predicting should sell Monday (5/4) or Tuesday (5/5). This is a fraction of the 11.07 it expects within 7 days and is a safe play with huge reward (+231, 17.6%).

Not using all 2,100 leaves room to invest in more after running today/tonight.

May 4, 2020

SELL Arconic (ARNC). BUY United Airlines (UAL).

Here are the projected percent growth changes with corresponding uncertainties given by the neural network.

From this we can see Arconic is no longer expected to grow, but is up 5% so we sell our 150 shares at $8.90 ($1,335). (+22.50, +1.71%)

The recommended purchase was AMD, but we were late to purchase. By the time the neural net finished, AMD was already at 52.56 (+5.37%) which is almost larger than the entire predicted gain (53.55).

Instead we bought the second choice, UAL, since it was actually down. We bought 20 shares at $25.38 ($507.6) which is a lower position since the uncertainty is high. It is expected to grow to 28.12 in the next 7 days. We put a limit sell in at 27.00 as a safe play with huge reward(+32.4, +6.4%).

We also missed OXY and BHF as they were up ~2% before the neural net finished.
