Digital Marketing: quick tips
for the early startup in 2019.

What's worked for me - Collaboarator and My Kee.

Investment Sites, Facebook, News, Google, Instagram, Spotify, and App Store Search

Investment Sites - F6S, Crunchbase

Have ideas? Generate a business model canvas for them and then make postings on F6S.

Want to find a startup?

Job postings on F6S that provide equity.

Facebook - Throttles Ads Based on Perpetual Budget - Expect to Keep Paying Right Now (use for single-purchase items not social outreach). Middle-Aged people.

First Ad (GIF with Boar doing animation): Spent $2.50 - 1,100 people reached

Fourth Ad (GIF with Boar doing animation): Spent $10.00 - 1,200 people reached

Promotes First Paid Ad Four times more than other ads.

Potentially setup a new account every time you post an ad? Harder to get text approved with new account.

Without boosting posts only reach about 10% of followers.

We have about 1100 followers and this was shared by 2 others each with about 4000 followers.

News - Local Television, Journals, Newspapers, and more

Simon Tam

Help a Reporter (HARO) - Hundreds of people looking to put your name on their news story.

You can tailor any product/expertise to any article.

This has worked for me as I've appeared on KOLR News, Mosource Link, and more.

Google - Pick a name that is searchable - Write Blog Posts on your website that match most searched keywords in Google. Everyone.

Keywords Anywhere -- Google Chrome Extension

Google Gives like $50 free for every $100 you spend.

My business name is atrocious. For searching and as an app name.

Instagram - Only platform to notify ALL followers you are live. Every post reaches all followers for free. Young, Female.

Rick Barker

Direct Messaging Personal Messages Is King (200 followers in 2 weeks).

Invite others to go live with you. It will notify all of their followers with you.

Spotify - Forced Ad Listen. Young, Male and Female.

Upload single image and audio track (ad).

App Stores - Zero Ads, just good listing name.

Basic Audio Editor - When people type in "basic " we appear. Hundreds of dollars in revenue with no ads.
